Company Overview:

Plinth Construction Limited is a dynamic and innovative indigenous real estate development, civil engineering, and general contracting firm. Our mission is to provide luxurious, smart, comfortable, and affordable homes in eco-friendly environments, equipped with modern infrastructure. We are committed to delivering high-quality projects with precision craftsmanship at competitive prices.

Our Expertise:

Plinth Construction Limited specializes in delivering affordable housing solutions across Nigeria. Our team comprises certified professionals with extensive industry experience, spanning eight (8) to twelve (12) years. Our experts have previously worked with renowned organizations such as E+D Consult, China Harbor Engineering Company, and Sterling Bank Plc, among others, holding positions from workmen to managerial levels.

Project Portfolio:

  • Construction of a 24-room transit camp for Rating at the Nigerian Defense Academy, Kaduna.
  • Construction of the Military/Para-Military factory for the Defense Industries Corporation of Nigeria (DICON-SUR), Kaduna.
  • Construction of a 576-meter pre-stressed bridge at the Lagos-Ibadan railway.
  • Development of 45 housing units at Kagara Estate in Kaduna Millennium City.
  • Construction of the Abuja International Conference Center, Asokoro.
  • Construction of the NIA Office, Asokoro, Abuja.
  • Ongoing development of KYC Housing Estate, Abuja.

Our Strengths and Value Proposition:

  • Structural integrity and durability in all projects.
  • Prevention of defects such as settlement issues, dampness, wall and floor cracks, and algae growth.
  • High precision and accuracy in construction, enhancing aesthetic appeal at optimal costs.

Our Offerings:

  • Sale of laid-out and bare land.
  • Construction of foundation structures.
  • Construction of concrete floors.
  • Full estate construction.
  • Development of essential estate infrastructure.
  • Sale of completed and uncompleted houses.
